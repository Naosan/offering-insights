Review brief

Category-specific reporting
Review question How do selected public videos compare across YouTube categories?

Compare a small, operator-selected cohort of videos and identify category, channel, and engagement signals for editorial planning.

Input boundary Operator-selected public video IDs, not keyword search.

The operator supplies known public video IDs or URLs. The prototype does not call `youtube.search.list` and does not collect private user data.

Report output Reusable observation note for review work.

The dashboard groups videos by category, adds public channel context, samples public playlist membership, and drafts a compact report.
